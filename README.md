Youdemy is an online learning platform designed to offer users access to a wide variety of courses on topics ranging from technology and business to arts and personal development.
Inspired by the concept of online education, Youdemy empowers learners of all ages and skill levels to learn new skills, advance their careers, or pursue personal interests—all from the comfort of their home.

The platform provides a modern, user-friendly interface for discovering courses, enrolling in them, and tracking learning progress. With a focus on accessibility and ease of use,
Youdemy aims to make high-quality education available to everyone, anytime, anywhere.

Key Features:
Course Catalog: Explore a diverse range of courses in various categories, such as Programming, Design, Marketing, Business, and more.
Course Details: View detailed information for each course, including instructor profiles, curriculum outlines, pricing, and user reviews.
User Registration & Authentication: Create an account to sign in and save your learning progress.
Course Enrollment: Enroll in any course that interests you and start learning at your own pace.
Progress Tracking: Keep track of your learning journey and monitor your progress through completed lessons and courses.
Responsive Design: The platform is fully responsive, offering an optimized experience on both desktop and mobile devices.

Technologies Used:
HTML5: For creating the structure and content of the pages.
CSS3: For styling and making the platform visually appealing.
JavaScript: For creating dynamic content, handling interactions, and ensuring a smooth user experience.

This description summarizes what Youdemy is all about—an accessible and user-friendly platform for online learning. It highlights the main features and technologies used to build the frontend of the platform.
